About Us

We are committed to excellence in education.

Vision Institute of Technology - Aligarh (VIT-A) is recognized by the AICTE and affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow (formerly UPTU) under AKTU Code 524. Additionally, VIT-Kanpur is affiliated with the Board of Technical Education, Lucknow, for Polytechnic (Diploma) programs (BTE Code 3343) and is also associated with the NCVT for ITI courses (PU09900776). Vision Institute of Technology (VIT), Aligarh, established in 2009, is a private engineering college approved by AICTE and affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow. Situated on a 25-acre campus along the Aligarh-Agra Highway, VIT offers undergraduate (B.Tech) and diploma (Polytechnic) programs in disciplines such as Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical & Electronics, Electronics & Communication, Agricultural Engineering, and emerging fields like Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. The institute emphasizes a blend of academic rigor and practical experience, supported by modern laboratories, experienced faculty, and a dedicated placement cell that facilitates industry connections and career opportunities for students.
